Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Sidra Wallet is a non-custodial wallet application designed for the SidraChain ecosystem. The application operates locally on the user's device and is designed to give users full control over their digital assets.

Information We Do Not Collect

Sidra Wallet does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share:

  • Private Keys
  • Recovery Phrases (Seed Phrases)
  • Wallet Passwords
  • Personal Identity Information
  • Financial Account Information
  • Biometric Information
  • Location Information
  • Advertising Identifiers

Local Device Storage

Wallet data required for application functionality may be stored locally on your device. This information remains under the user's control and is not transmitted to the developer.

Blockchain Data

Transactions created through Sidra Wallet are broadcast to the SidraChain network. Wallet addresses, transaction hashes, balances, and transaction history may be publicly visible through blockchain explorers and network nodes.

DApp Connections

Sidra Wallet may allow connections to third-party decentralized applications (DApps). Users interact directly with those services and are subject to their own privacy policies and terms.

No User Accounts

Sidra Wallet does not require registration and does not provide user accounts.

No Analytics or Tracking

Sidra Wallet does not collect analytics data, advertising identifiers, usage tracking information, or personal information.

Security

Users are solely responsible for protecting their recovery phrases, private keys, passwords, and device security. Loss of recovery phrases or private keys may result in permanent loss of access to digital assets.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
